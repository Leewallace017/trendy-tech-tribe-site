**Editorial Guidelines:**
- Take a clear position but back it with evidence
- Acknowledge legitimate counterarguments
- Avoid personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Focus on ideas and implications, not personalities
- Ensure all factual claims are verifiable
- Aim for 1200-2000 words
- Be provocative but not reckless
