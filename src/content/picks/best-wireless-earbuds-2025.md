## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are expensive earbuds worth it over $50 options?**
A: If you use earbuds daily, yes. Premium models offer noticeably better sound quality, ANC, battery life, and build quality. But $80 options like Anker Space A40 deliver solid performance if budget is tight.

**Q: How important is codec support?**
A: LDAC (Sony, Anker) or aptX (Bose) deliver better sound quality than standard AAC, but only if your phone supports them. iPhone users are limited to AAC regardless of earbuds.

**Q: Can I use these for workouts?**
A: All options listed have IPX4 or better water resistance, sufficient for sweat. For running, look for secure fit (Bose with stability bands) or true sport-focused models.

**Q: How long do wireless earbuds last before breaking?**
A: Batteries degrade over 2-3 years of daily use. Premium brands (Sony, Apple, Bose) tend to last longer than budget options. Expect 2-4 years of reliable use.
