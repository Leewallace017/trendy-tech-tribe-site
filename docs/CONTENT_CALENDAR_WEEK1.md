2. **Verify facts** across 3+ reputable sources
3. **Get real quotes** from actual people
4. **Use actual statistics** from verified sources
5. **Link to real articles** (not made-up URLs)

### Source Quality Standards

**Acceptable sources:**
- ✅ Major news outlets (CNBC, Bloomberg, Reuters, CNN, Washington Post)

- ✅ Official company announcements
- ✅ Government agencies (EIA, DOE, IEA)

**Unacceptable:**
